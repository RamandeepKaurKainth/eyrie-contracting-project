Who We Are

Eyrie Contracting Inc. is a full‑service general contracting company delivering high‑quality construction, welding, and fabrication services across British Columbia.

With expertise in carpentry, steelwork, HVAC, foundations, roofing, flooring, and project management, we bring precision and reliability to every project — residential, commercial, or industrial.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional construction and fabrication services built on integrity, quality, and community. We approach every project with craftsmanship, safety, and transparency.

From planning to execution, we prioritize communication, safety, and excellence — ensuring every build stands the test of time.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Eyrie Contracting has grown into a trusted provider of construction, welding, and fabrication services across BC.

With over 25 years of combined experience, our team brings deep industry knowledge and a commitment to excellence.

Our reputation is built on precision, safety, and craftsmanship — values that guide every project we take on.

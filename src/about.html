Who We Are

Eyrie Contracting Inc. is a full-service general contracting company dedicated to delivering high-quality workmanship, structural integrity, and reliable project coordination. From residential construction to commercial renovations and industrial support, our team brings years of experience across carpentry, fabrication, HVAC, steelwork, foundations, roofing, flooring, and project management.

We combine expert craftsmanship with modern building practices and transparent communication — ensuring every project is delivered with care and built to last.

Our Mission

At Eyrie Contracting Inc., we are committed to delivering exceptional construction, welding, and fabrication services with a foundation built on integrity, quality, and community. We take pride in every project, big or small, and approach each one with a dedication to craftsmanship and safety. Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our clients by understanding their needs, exceeding their expectations, and contributing positively to the communities we serve. Through collaboration, innovation, and a strong commitment to ethical practices, we strive to make a meaningful impact in every project we undertake.

From planning to execution, we prioritize integrity, safety, communication, and excellence — delivering projects that stand the test of time.

Our History

Eyrie Contracting Inc. was founded in 2018 and quickly established itself as a trusted provider of construction, welding, and fabrication services throughout British Columbia. With over 25 years of combined industry experience, our team delivers deep expertise and a strong commitment to excellence in every project.

From residential and commercial builds to specialized welding, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality results with precision, integrity, and reliability.

Our attention to detail, strong safety culture, and dedication to craftsmanship have earned us long-standing trust from clients and the respect of peers across the industry. As we grow, we remain focused on delivering exceptional service, building strong relationships, and contributing to the success of every community we serve.

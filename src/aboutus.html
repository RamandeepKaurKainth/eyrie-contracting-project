Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our project types, delivery approach, safety standards, and construction expertise.

What types of construction projects do you specialize in?

A: We specialize in commercial, industrial, and institutional construction projects, with a focus on complex, high-value builds ranging from $3M to $100M. Our expertise includes tenant improvements, large-scale expansions, and design-build delivery.

Do you offer design-build construction services?

A: Yes, we provide integrated design-build services, managing projects from initial concept through to completion. This approach improves efficiency, reduces risk, and ensures alignment between design, budget, and construction.

Do you work with municipal and institutional clients?

A: Absolutely. We regularly coordinate with municipalities, including the City of Vancouver, as well as institutional stakeholders. We are experienced in navigating permitting, inspections, and regulatory compliance requirements.

What is your typical project size?

A: Our core focus is on projects valued between $3 million and $100 million, allowing us to deliver the level of planning, coordination, and execution required for complex builds.

How do you ensure projects stay on schedule and on budget?

A: We implement rigorous pre-construction planning, cost control systems, and detailed scheduling, supported by experienced project management teams. Early risk identification and proactive coordination are key to maintaining timelines and budgets.

Can you complete work in occupied or operational facilities?

A: Yes. We specialize in phased construction within active environments, ensuring minimal disruption to ongoing operations while maintaining safety and productivity.

What safety standards do you follow?

A: We adhere to all applicable WorkSafeBC regulations and maintain comprehensive safety programs tailored to each project. Safety is integrated into every phase of planning and execution.

How do you manage permits and inspections?

A: We coordinate all aspects of permitting, inspections, and approvals, working closely with local authorities such as the City of Vancouver to ensure full compliance and timely approvals.

What makes your company different from other general contractors?

A: Our approach combines high-level project management, technical expertise, and a focus on complex, large-scale construction. We prioritize long-term client relationships and consistently deliver projects that meet demanding operational and regulatory requirements.

How early should we engage a general contractor?

A: Engaging a contractor during the concept or pre-construction phase provides the greatest value. Early involvement allows for accurate budgeting, constructability input, and streamlined project delivery.
